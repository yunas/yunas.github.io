We’re An Awesome Amazon Fire Application Development Company

We’ve already conquered iPhone and Android, so what’s next for us? Amazon Fire. The sleek phone by Amazon has an unrivaled potential that other new mobile platforms just haven’t been able to muster. As an Amazon Fire apps developer, we know that apps on this platform will have a different aesthetic than has been seen before and tilt controls that no other OS has even reached.

What is Fueled?

Fueled is a strategic app design and development agency, with offices spread out in New York, Chicago, and London. We’ve been making iPhone and Android apps from the ground up since 2009 and are able to bring an international perspective by having such a global team across the board. It’s safe to say that the exceptional design and development accolades we’ve earned will easily transfer to the Amazon Fire phone. We work with everyone from startups to enterprises to somewhere in between.

We’ve made well known apps such as Discovery Communications, JackThreads, and Kapture. With our help, businesses will have an easy time of transitioning onto this contemporary platform for their new and current Fire apps. Users should feel like they can’t get through the day without your app, regardless of what operating system it’s built on.

Our clients and users are thrilled with our products; the apps we’ve built have generated $250 million in revenue and that number continues to grow every day.

How We Develop Amazon Fire Apps

With new features like Dynamic Display, allowing users to engage with their phones by tilting it in various direction, and amazing camera, sporting 13 MP rear facing camera with optical image stabilization, there are endless possibilities. Developing and designing Amazon Fire apps will follow the same process that our others do.

The design team will hash out the details of your idea and turn it into a workable app. Every feature and function will be detailed before it moves on to the design and development team. Backend coding and visual design are done at the same time to make sure that the code lines up with the designs.

We make sure to keep our clients informed of how their project is going through the agile development process. The work is done in quick, two work bursts and the progress is presented to the client biweekly. This may seem like a long stretch of time, but our team of designers and developers is able to work this way efficiently.
