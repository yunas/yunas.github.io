Robert Minton

Robert Minton has a successful track record working at major US media companies in the roles of sales, product development, marketing, and general management. He has worked for companies such as Walt Disney, ABC, and directly supported ABC News and ESPN Audio as their distribution and sales partner. Robert has lead numerous successful business development opportunities, working with partners like Nielsen, Terra.com, Sustainlane.com, Marketron Mobile, Abacast, and Comcast Entertainment (now NBCU). He has also provided consulting services on the topic of integrated media services and M&A's to industry leaders such as McKinsey & Co., Bain & Co., as well as other major US investment groups. Robert has a BA from the University of Tennessee and has participated in executive education programs at Columbia Business School, UCLA - Anderson School of Business, and Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth. He brings over 17 years of management and business experience to Six Apart.
