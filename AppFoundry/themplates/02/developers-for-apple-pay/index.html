Apple Pay

When Apple announced the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus in 2014, they promised a new feature called Apple Pay. The handy system allows iPhone users to pay for goods directly from their phones, without ever having to take out your credit card and it’s built with state of the art safety features so you can pay for your merchandise without worrying about credit card theft.

Big companies such as Target and McDonald’s have already signed-up to use this exquisite service with more and more keying into the app’s potential.

With the myriad issues plaguing businesses and their credit card systems, adopting Apple Pay could potentially save you a big headache. Best of all, it is convenient and comforting to your customers, mobile veterans and newbies alike.
