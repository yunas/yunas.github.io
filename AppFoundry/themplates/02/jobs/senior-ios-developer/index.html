Senior iOS Developer

New York or London

Start Date: Immediately

What We're Up To

We're leading the charge in a world of apps that do stuff, and do it with style. We work with our clients--from small startups to big brands--to figure out how to achieve their marketing and business goals through smart, high-functioning apps and websites that genuinely engage theirs users and keep them coming back for more. We also run one of the top coworking spaces in Manhattan, located in the heart of Soho and recently featured in The New York Times. The Fueled Collective is home to over 30 startups, and boasts a well-stocked snack wall, a ping pong table, and incredible views of the Manhattan skyline.

Where You Fit In

You are an experienced iOS developer with a strong background working with Objective-C, and some leadership experience. You will drive the development of apps that embody Fueled's commitment to building excellent products. You must understand mobile software design and development at an experienced level and be willing and able to lead individual, motivated development teams. You will love working with a team of highly skilled people, whether they are in the office next to you or working remotely. As a member of the Fueled team you must be inspired by technology and have a passion for creating innovative technical solutions in a fast, energetic environment.

The Ideal Candidate Has:

  • BA/BS in Computer Science or related field
  • 2+ years experience in iOS application development
  • 2+ years experience in a leadership role
  • Programming experience in: Java, Objective-C, .NET and/or C++
  • Experience interacting with external REST APIs
  • Experience working with external frameworks and libraries
  • Experience with agile and lean processes
  • Must have excellent problem solving skills and love technical challenges
  • Must be able to take initiative to be productive and efficient
