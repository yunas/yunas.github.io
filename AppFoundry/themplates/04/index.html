About us

Carbon Studio is a creative agency with a love for all things digital. Founded in 2007 our philosophy has remained the same throughout- create award winning experiences for our clients and their customers. Simple!

What makes us different? We treat each project as unique, ensuring a bespoke solution to every brief. And whilst we always ensure we make use of the latest technologies in our development, we don't forget our roots- great design, attention to detail and a creative approach to the digital space. 

And what else makes us tick? Music, cycling, tea and beer!
